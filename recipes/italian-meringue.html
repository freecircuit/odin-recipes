Itanlian Meringue

Description

Italian Meringue is a combination of eggwhites and boiling sugar resulting in a delicious marshmello like topping to our lemon-meringue tart.

Ingredients

  • 1lb egg-whites
  • 2lb sugar
  • 1cup water
  • 1/2tsp cream of tartar
  • few drops of vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Place egg-whites, cream of tartar, and vanilla extract in mixing bowl
  2. Place bowl on mixer and run at high-speed with whisk attatment
  3. While egg-whites are mixing combine sugar and water in a pot
  4. place candy thermometer in mixture and bring to 250 degrees F
  5. Once at 250degrees, pour contents of pot into mixing bowl while continually mixing
  6. The sugar-mixture should cook the egg-whites, allow to mix for 10min
  7. Move mernigue from bowl into storage container, refridgerate for 3 hours
  8. When ready, place contents back in mixing bowl and whisk at high speed until stiff peaks, about 10min
