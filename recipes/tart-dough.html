Tart Dough

Description

Sweet tart dough (Pâte Sucrée) is the base of and sweet tart. We will be using it as the container for our lemon-meringue tart.

Ingredients

  • Room-Temperature Butter
  • Powdered Sugar
  • Pastry Flour
  • Salt
  • Eggs

Steps

  1. Combine butter, sugar, salt in bowl and place on mixer at low speed
  2. Once incorporated add the flour and continue to mix, about 2min
  3. Once fully incorporated, dig a well to bottom of bowl and add the Eggs
  4. Mix until the dough comes together in a smooth consistency
  5. Move from bowl to saran wrap, and allow to set in fridge for a few hours
  6. Once set it is ready to b e rolled out and fit into tart shell
